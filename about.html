Our Journey

How We Started

The story of SHIMOGY — from an idea to a growing community

Early 2024

The Idea Is Born

A small group of friends in Kawempe started discussing how they could pool resources to invest together rather than individually. The name "SHIMOGY" was created from the founders' vision.

Mid 2024

First Members Join

The first few members joined with small contributions. We started with a simple savings cycle (GROUP 1) where members contributed UGX 3,000 each.

Late 2024

First Investment Plans

We launched our first investment opportunities — the Men's Hair Salon and Modern Stall plans. These small-scale investments gave members a taste of earning returns.

Early 2025

Growing Slowly

Word spread in Kawempe and more people joined. We added Poultry Farm and Restaurant plans. The group grew to about 15 active members.

2026

Going Digital

We launched this website to make it easier for members to browse plans, submit requests, and stay updated. We're now serving members across Kawempe and beyond.
